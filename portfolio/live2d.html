Live2D Models

Every time I touch Live2D I feel my life force draining into my neon piss green HP Pavilion Gaming Laptop. When I open up Live2D I am faced with the dread of the texture atlas and my eyes sink into my skull as I set params. I am nothing. We are nothing. But Live2D is eternal. XD but itz soo funny to make ur model wiggle!!!

I created this model in celebration of my first birthday with my darling goats! This model uses the original Chupi model as a base, but with updated rigging, new props, and a special dance animation. The rigging was a step up from the original Chupi model, which I understood would be a requirement in illustration of the model.

This is Pixl! A Lampong and my first ever client! Her model was conceptualized, illustrated, and rigged by me, and she was the first model i worked on while on stream! Additionally this is my first model that includes multiple animations and complex layering. Thank you Pixl for becoming my first vtuber child.

The wonderful Miss Luna was illustrated by me, and my first formal commission! Her strong punk design was a fun challenge to model. The intense layering was one of the most difficult things to work with, so I used the experience to try new layering techniques for parts that I struggled with in the past. Currently, her rigging process is set to take place over the next month before her debut!

This is Chupi's default model! As my first foray into the world of Live2D modeling, she was a true labor of love. I poured my heart and soul into her creation, teaching myself the ins and outs of rigging through countless sleepless nights and weeks of intense concentration.
